The Point Apartments

About:
Welcome to our Capstone project. Let me introduce you to The Point Apartments.

​The project aimed to design a mixed-use residential building to respect and enhance the existing community and its best-loved features. Accommodate the change occurring, fulfill this city's strategic vision, and incorporate a sense of connection between the residents and the public.

The Point Apartments are comprised of 45 residential suites and provide its residents with an amenity space on the fourth level and a living street inspired by Dutch Urban Design on the Northside of the building.

​The building required parking is provided in a one-level underground parkade. Facing the public streets are 5 designated retail suites that can be seen from the LRT station.

​This 10,000 square meter, four-storey, Mixed-Use Residential building comprises mass timber CLT panels, glulam columns, beams and concrete elements.

​The project is located in the community of Shaganappi Point, 5 minutes west of downtown Calgary. A neighbourhood with unique homes and stunning views of the Shaganappi Golf course.

This Project was awarded first place in the Alberta Masonry Councils Award.

Job Role:
I am working alongside two other team members to deliver drawing packages from schematic design to the construction document phase. My role has been researching the communities needs and creatively solving any challenges that have arisen during the production of this project.
